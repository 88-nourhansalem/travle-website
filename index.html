Top Recommendations

Beach Recommendation — Maldives

White sands, turquoise waters — perfect for relaxation and snorkeling.

Highlights: Overwater bungalows, snorkeling with reef fish, tranquil sunsets.

Best months: November — April.

Temple Recommendation — Angkor Wat (Cambodia)

Historic temples and stunning sunrise views over the moats and towers.

Highlights: Ancient stone carvings, sunrise at the main temple, nearby markets and food stalls.

Tips: Hire a local guide and arrive early for sunrise crowds.

Recommendation by Country — Japan

Culture, cuisine, and modern-meets-traditional experiences across cities and countryside.

Highlights: Food markets, temples, efficient rail system, cherry blossoms (seasonal).

Suggested route: Tokyo → Kyoto → Osaka → Hiroshima.
